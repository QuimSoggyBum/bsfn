### Please Take a Moment to Understand Before Reporting
For a larger read up on this section please read Regressia's [GitHub](https://github.com/RegressiaUK/NappyCraftLinks/blob/main/platform_reporting.md) version

Regressia kindly ask that you take the time to understand our's & their work before choosing to report it. Our's and their mods was created for people including children who have gone through difficult or traumatic experiences early in life, when their minds were still learning how to understand the world.

Regression has an important place in healing and self-expression, especially in games where it’s easier to relax and act younger without the stigma of “acting your age.” I personally experienced several forms of abuse at a young age, and much of what I include in this project is based on genuine medical treatments I received growing up. The mod draws inspiration from the UK’s NHS healthcare system, while the term TAR (Therapeutic Age Regression) comes from US medical terminology but is still understood in the UK.

When you falsely report these mod, you’re not only causing unnecessary strain on the platform but you’re also taking away something that truly helps people. Yes, some of those people are children. Imagine a young person who wants to safely relive a comforting part of their childhood within Minecraft, because they can’t talk about it openly at school. When you report and remove that opportunity, you take away one of the few safe outlets they have.

Please don’t misjudge what we’re doing. Regressia's project is not connected to the ABDL,DDLG and Age Play communities and any other Adult Related communities. Regressia and myself Fay are incontinent and we don’t enjoy needing to wear protection, but we do believe it’s important to represent medical realities with dignity and not ridicule. Unfortunately, some groups treat these issues as jokes, which makes it harder for real people with medical needs to feel seen and respected.

In Regressia's mod, items like protection have practical in-game benefits (for example, slow falling), allowing players to relax and enjoy the experience without worry. That’s the purpose for comfort, inclusion, and gentle healing through play.
